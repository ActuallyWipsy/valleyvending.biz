Provide your employees with a perk

From coffee to snacks and drinks, we have your back. Companies that offer Office Refreshment Service (ORS) have higher employee satisfaction and lower turnover rates. Energized by the evolving spaces where people work, meet, and dine, we give you more than just beverages, curated coffee service, water filtration, light snacks, and full meals - we offer inspired break experiences that foster a true sense of inclusivity and community.

We Set-Up

We bring our top-of-the-line equipment to your business on a lease or own basis. We bring the initial stock of supplies (coffee, snacks, beverages) and provide instructions as needed.

We Maintain

If any issues arise, we are a phone call away from setting up an appointment to have a service technician visit your business and make any repairs as needed.

We Re-Stock

On schedule or as supplies run low, we drop off supplies to keep your employees in great shape. When re-stocking, we do a quick inspection of the equipment and clean it as well.
